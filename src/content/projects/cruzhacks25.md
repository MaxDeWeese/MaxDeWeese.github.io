## Overview

Sustain the Earth is a tile-based strategy game developed in 3 days for CruzHacks 2025. Players strategically place structures on a grid to generate electricity and reduce emissions, balancing limited resources to prevent energy shortages while keeping global emissions below critical levels.

Tech used:
- Unity Game Engine
- C# 
- Pixel Art
